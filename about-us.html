Luminate's Mission

To be the future of learning and the beginning of a change

We think everyone should enjoy learning and should always be looking forward to learn new things.

Our Team believes that learning should not be school bound and that we should always be ready to develop ourselves constantly. And learning should not be something that is limited & boring.

The Luminate Team wants you to be apart of our family , where we come together as a community to help each & everyone grow. And as a team , we will help everyone understand that learning is not boring. In fact , It will make you hungry for more knowledge once you get into it!

So, What is Luminate?

Luminate is a platform where individuals can take up quizzes to broaden their knowledge on various areas. They will also be allowed to create quizzes for others to take up. Individuals will definietly be motivated with our gamification features! Luminate wants each of you to get the knowledge you deserve.
