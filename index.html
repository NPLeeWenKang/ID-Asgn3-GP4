Responsive Design

Our Web Application is designed to work on various devices such as Desktops , Tablets and Mobile devices.

Our Team's Idea is simple. We want our application to provide good user experience and for our visitors to access and navigate our website with ease.

Gamification

Gamification in web design seeks to include elements of games such as earning coins and purchasing items from shop to improve users quality of learning.

This is an evolving method to increase the motivation and involvement of users by integrating elements of game design into educational environments.
